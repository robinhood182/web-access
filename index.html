Robyn Navarro

I am a full-stack developer with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VueJS, ReactJS, Redux, NodeJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, Mongoose and PostgreSQL currently residing in the beautiful city of Portland. Before I realized that I wanted to become a developer, I worked with Walt Disney World as one of their managers at the All-Star Resorts, leading a group of 20+ employees.

Though a people-person by nature, my previous role as leader taught me much about how to relate to others and get their best side to show. I have a passion for learning and think you can always learn something from anyone; when someone wants to share, I do my best to listen. I'm a careful thinker, especially when it comes to finding solutions, always considering all contributing factors before making important decisions.

I am a kind and considerate person with a positive mindset. I enjoy exploring the city with my partner and our dog Puchungo, playing video games, solving riddles, eating good food (which I sometimes cook), listening to my favorite podcasts and traveling.
